<p>This <a href="https://sorse.github.io/" target="_blank">SORSE</a>&nbsp;talk, delivered with KDL Deputy Director Arianna Ciula,&nbsp;describes King&rsquo;s Digital Lab (KDL), a Research Software Engineering lab operating within the Faculty of Arts and Humanities at King&rsquo;s College London (UK).&nbsp; The KDL team of 18 project managers, analysts, designers, engineers, and systems managers specialise in arts &amp; humanities, cultural heritage, and creative industries research and development. The talk provides a current state overview of the lab, and describes our RSE HR roles (see <a href="https://zenodo.org/record/2564790" target="_blank">https://zenodo.org/record/2564790</a>) and a relatively recent trial initiative that defines the different ways the team can contribute to research. A more technically-oriented overview of our ongoing work in refining the fundamental components of KDL Software Development Lifecycle (see https://github.com/kingsdigitallab/sdlc-for-rse/) is also&nbsp;provided, including descriptions of our modelling workflows, processes, and efforts to increase sustainability, reproducibility, and research data management best practice (<a href="https://zenodo.org/record/3361580#.X09x6mdKhhE" target="_blank">https://zenodo.org/record/3361580#.X09x6mdKhhE</a>).</p>
