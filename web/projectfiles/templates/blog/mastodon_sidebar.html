I highly recommend the recent 'Ethics of Decentralized Social Technologies' white paper: https://gettingplurality.org/2023/03/18/ethics-of-decentralized-social-technologies-lessons-from-the-web3-wave/.

One in a series of papers from the GETTING-Plurality Research Network. I'm taken by their broad approach, balancing political economy & philosophy with solid socio-technical analysis.
